# Do'raya the Khajiit Barbarian

A Khajiit follower for Skyrim.

A barbarian from Anequina, Do'raya seeks to test her guile and ferocity on the savage lands of Skyrim.

## Recruiting

Do'raya is a member of [Ma'dran's caravan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27dran) that travels between Windhelm and Solitude. She will ask you to kill the leader of a bandit camp in either Eastmarch or Haafingar. After completing her quest, Do'raya will train you in Two Handed and Sneak and may be recruited afterwards.

## Do'raya's skills

- Two Handed
- Archery
- Block
- Light Armour
- Sneak

Do'raya has the Light Foot, Barbarian (Rank 1), and Agile Defender (Rank 1) perks.

## Tech

- Uses the default follower system.
- Is marked essential.
- Can be married.
- Uses your own body models and textures.

## Requirements

- [Fuz Ro D-oh - Silent Voice](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/15109) for the unvoiced dialogue
- Either [Missing Follower Dialogue Edit](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/56115) or [Relationship Dialogue Overhaul - Update and MCM](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/44601)
